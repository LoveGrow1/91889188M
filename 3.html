Your partner's
unseen needs

A need for joint decision making:

While you feel that you make choices beneficial for the both of you, your partner might not see it that way. She might need to feel more included and have a say in significant decisions affecting you both.

A need for confidence when expressing desires:

Your partner seems to be uncomfortable communicating her wants and needs for fear of your possible reaction. It's crucial to create an atmosphere where she feels free to voice her desires.

A need for more empathy during emotional distress:

Your partner might be feeling that there are times when her emotions aren't fully understood or addressed, leading her to feel a bit neglected emotionally.
